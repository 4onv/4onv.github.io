The Issue

The unmarked burial sites and undocumented deaths of Indigenous children at residential schools are a tragic legacy of historical injustices and systemic failures.

Get Help

  • Indian Residential Schools Crisis Line
  • Assembly of First Nations
  • Indigenous Services Canada
