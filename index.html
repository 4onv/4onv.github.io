Honoring the Lost Indigenous Children

History, Context, and Reconciliation

Residential schools were a tragic chapter in Canada’s history, where Indigenous children were forcibly removed from their families and placed in institutions designed to assimilate them. The trauma from these experiences persists, impacting generations of Indigenous communities. It’s crucial to understand this history, honor the victims, and commit to reconciliation to build a better future.
